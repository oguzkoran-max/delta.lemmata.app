## Start Findings

- P004 payload-free state cannot provide P007 raw text; validated bytes need a
  direct P003-to-P005 private prepare-only handoff.
- P006 can prove calculation from counts but not that counts came from one accepted
  text profile; a receipt-bound P007 builder must close that gap without changing
  the P006 wire schema.
- Corpus health is bypassable unless one mandatory READY admission facade owns the
  public P005/P006 enqueue path.
- P003 whitespace token totals are intake statistics, not stylometric prepared-token
  totals.
- P004 v1 lacks segment/parent-work, OCR, paratext, and analysis-role assertions;
  add a versioned overlay instead of rewriting accepted P004 evidence.
- The proposed profile preserves diacritics and stopwords, uses Unicode lowercase
  plus NFC and surface-word tokens, and disables automatic OCR correction,
  lemmatization, modernization, and paratext removal.
- Near-duplicate, shared-passage, minimum-data, length, and group thresholds are
  human-owned method decisions. They remain proposed.
- P007 connects CE-02, CE-04, CE-09 and EPI-01, EPI-02, EPI-04, EPI-06, EPI-07,
  EPI-11.
