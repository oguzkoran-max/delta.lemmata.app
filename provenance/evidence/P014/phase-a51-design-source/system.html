Panel, table, and band borders (#e2e5e4 / #eef0ef) are decorative: the adjacent fill, heading, and text identify those regions, so WCAG 2.2 SC 1.4.11 imposes no minimum on them. Boundaries that are required to identify a control meet 3:1 via the control-line token. Reference: W3C, Understanding SC 1.4.11 Non-text Contrast (WCAG 2.2).
